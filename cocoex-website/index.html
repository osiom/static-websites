unleashing the compounds of existence through Art, Impact, and Community

cocoex is a social impact lab that builds the platform and facilitates the conditions for art and community to generate real-world change.

Comet Collab is the ecosystem — the hemisphere where artists, collectors, activists, and citizens converge through Stardust and Horizon, guided by Muse, in a continuous loop of creation and impact.

our constellation. Seven Muses, seven causes is the curatorial compass that runs through everything cocoex does.

Each Muse embodies one of seven global challenges (inspired by the UN SDGs) where collective creative effort can generate meaningful systemic change.

Lunes

Mystery and intuition flow through Lunes, reflecting the cyclical nature of existence.

Ares

A force of passion and courage, Ares channels the energy of transformation and bold action.

Rabu

Communication and connection thrive with Rabu, bridging worlds through expression.

Thunor

Thunder and strength converge in Thunor, a guardian of power and resilience.

Shukra

Beauty and harmony radiate from Shukra, inspiring creativity and connection.

Dosei

Wisdom and structure manifest through Dosei, anchoring dreams into reality.

Solis

The radiant embodiment of warmth and vitality, Solis brings light to the darkest corners of the universe.

Stardust - Artists select a cause, create a work, and launch a fundraising campaign through its sale — funds split between artist and organisation, facilitated by Cocoex. The artist is the vehicle.

Horizon - A Future Lab where communities collectively design their futures — moving through four steps from Critique to Realisation, transforming shared vision into art and the funds it raises into real-world change.
